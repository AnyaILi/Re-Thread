ABOUT US

We are a student led initiative to cut down the overconsumption seen throughout the modern world.
Our thrift store aims to reuse, recycle, and renew, inspiring a culture of sustainability across Valley Christian High School. By giving pre-loved items a second life, we not only reduce waste but also promote mindful consumption. Let's bring green to Valley together.
