Student spotlight: the team behind Re-Thread

Meet the volunteers who sort donations, style outfits, and set up each pop-up. They juggle classes and clubs while keeping the shop running smoothly.

From photography to merchandising, every role is student-led. Their favorite part? Seeing a piece they processed find a new owner the same day.
