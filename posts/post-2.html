How donating keeps clothing out of landfills

Every hoodie, tee, and jacket you donate gets a second story on campus. We sort items for quality, clean what needs a refresh, and photograph pieces so shoppers can spot what they want fast.

Last semester alone we diverted dozens of bags of clothes from the trash, keeping textiles out of landfills and extending their life with new owners.

Want to contribute? Drop-off bins are outside the student center all week. We love denim, basics, outerwear, and school-appropriate tops.
